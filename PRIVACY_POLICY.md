# Privacy Policy (Kora Tuning Companion)

Effective date: 2026-02-14

Kora Tuning Companion ("the app") is designed to work offline. This policy explains what data the app uses and how it is handled.

## Summary

- The app does **not** collect or sell personal data.
- Microphone access is used for the tuner and is processed **on-device**.
- Tuning presets, instrument settings, and imported scores are processed **locally** on your device.
- The app does not require an internet connection and does not include third-party analytics/ads SDKs.

## Data the app uses

### Microphone audio (tuner)

If you enable the tuner, the app requests microphone permission (`RECORD_AUDIO`) so it can detect pitch.

- Audio is processed in real time on your device to calculate pitch/cents.
- The app does not record, store, or upload microphone audio.

### Local app data (presets/settings)

The app stores your selected instrument configuration, intonation offsets, and any user presets on your device so they persist between sessions.

- This data remains local to your device.
- You can delete custom presets inside the app. You can remove all local app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage through your operating system.
- Local data is kept until you delete it, clear app storage, or uninstall the app.

### Imported score files and exports

The notation tools can open user-selected MusicXML, MXL, MIDI, or supported PDF files and generate local outputs such as tablature, MIDI, WAV, or PDF exports.

- Imported files are processed on-device.
- The app does not upload imported files or generated exports.
- Exported files are created only when you choose to export or share them.
- If you share an export with another app, that transfer is controlled by your explicit share action and the receiving app's privacy practices.

## Sharing of data

The app does not share your data with third parties.

## Tracking, ads, and analytics

The app does not use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, cookies, or cross-app tracking.

## Retention and deletion

Local settings and presets are retained only on your device for app functionality. They can be deleted by deleting custom presets, clearing app storage, or uninstalling the app. The app does not maintain server-side accounts or server-side user data.

## Children’s privacy

The app is not directed to children and is not designed to collect personal information from children.

## Changes to this policy

If the app’s data practices change, this policy will be updated.
